Description

Yet another soup. Surprised? You shouldn't be. By now, you must've realised that we really love soup. What's there not to love? Easy to make and easy to eat and easy on your fat round belly. Anyways, this one has a little more meat than the other recipes, and also has some spice to it. So get ready your taste buds!

With a prep time of 15 minutes, and a cooking time of 25 minutes, this is for the chicken-lovers who also love their time, money and energy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1(28 ounces) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(10.5 ounces) can condensed chicken broth
  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • 2 teaspoons chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1(15 ounces) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 large boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cutinto bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup whole corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 cup white hominy,whatever that is
  • 1(4 ounces) can chopped green chile peppers
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ cup crushed tortilla chips, or to taste
  • 2 medium avocados, sliced, or to taste
  • ½ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in black beans, cooked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into individual serving bowls, and top with crushed tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and green onions.
